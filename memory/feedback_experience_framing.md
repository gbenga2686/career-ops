Always use "4+ years" for DevOps/SRE-specific experience — NOT "5+", "8+", or "8.5 years".

**Why:** The user corrected this directly. DevOps/SRE roles start at Huawei DevOps (Jun 2021). Total IT career including network engineering is ~8.5 years, but that is NOT the DevOps experience figure to use in CV summaries or cover letters.

**How to apply:**
- CV Professional Summary: "4+ years of DevOps/SRE experience" (or "cloud-native experience")
- Cover letter: "In 4+ years across DevOps and SRE roles..."
- Do NOT use "8+ years" as a DevOps figure, even when trying to bridge the experience gap for senior roles
- You MAY reference total infrastructure career separately if relevant (e.g., "with a broader background in infrastructure and network engineering dating to 2017"), but keep this secondary and only use it when it genuinely adds context
